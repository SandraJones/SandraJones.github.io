My Story

Github LinkedIn

I studied Chemical Technology in college and received an A.A.S. degree in Chemical Technology. While working full-time, I finished my B.S.B.A. degree, majoring in Finance. Through the years following graduation, I worked in accounting, supervisory, and management roles that I thoroughly enjoyed, although never working directly in finance. These positions enabled me to hone my supervisory and management skills.

I decided to enroll in Nashville Software School to enlarge my skill set and add tech skills to my portfolio. While at NSS, I completed a front-end and a back-end capstone so that I could work full stack development under the supervision of senior developers when I graduated.

I was able to secure my first software engineer position and enjoyed it, but the long daily commute (3 hours) was incompatible with my family's needs. So I took a break to manage the remodel of our two homes. During this time, I have networked, attended conferences, (Agile, Security, etc.), as well as starting to work freelance.

Some of my favorite non-work activities are playing cards and board games, crafting (card creation, scrapbooking and quilting), walking, travel, and reading non-fiction. My passion is politics, so I read extensively about current events. I maintain a website as webmaster for a local charity, Angels of Heart. The mission is close to my heart.
